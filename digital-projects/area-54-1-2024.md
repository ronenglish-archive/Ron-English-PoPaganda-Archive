# 2024 – *Ron English — Area 54 #1* (Digital Comic)

**Year:** 2024 (October 16, 2024)  
**Platform:** VeVe – Digital Comics  
**Type:** Origin-story digital comic (blind-box, multiple rarities)

![Ron English — Area 54 #1](../images/Digital/area54.webp)

## Overview

**“Area 54 #1”** introduces the origin story of two major Ron English characters—**Orion the Alien** and **MC Supersized**—within a dedicated narrative universe that merges pop surrealism, conspiracy culture, and English’s long-running Delusionville mythos.

Released as a blind-box NFT comic, the drop includes multiple cover variants ranging from common to ultra-rare, each reinterpreting Area 54’s lore cues: neon abduction lights, government redactions, pop-culture mashups, and the playful paranoia central to English’s extraterrestrial narratives.

Collectors see *Area 54 #1* as a major expansion point, cementing English’s sci-fi worldbuilding inside the VeVe comic ecosystem and bridging the Delusionville storyline with outer-space satire.
