# 2021 – *Be The Light* (Lightcult Crypto Club)

**Year:** 2021 (announced November 17, 2021)  
**Platform:** Lightcult Crypto Club  
**Type:** Large-scale generative NFT project (10,008 tokens)

![Be The Light — Lightcult Crypto Club](../images/Digital/bethelight.webp)

## Overview

**Be The Light** is a massive generative NFT project built around **10,008 unique “light bulb” characters**, each constructed from visual components drawn from Ron English’s four decades of POPaganda imagery. The series was developed as the **Lightcult Crypto Club**, merging street culture, surrealism, and digital collectibles into a single metaverse-ready identity project.

The light bulb serves as a universal Popaganda symbol—referencing ideas, warnings, commercial signage, and the aesthetics of mass production. English’s design language permeates every variant: from Delusionville creatures to advertising mascots and radical cartoon hybrids.  
The drop received significant attention from art and streetwear media, positioning the Lightcult project as one of English’s most ambitious digital expansions.
