# 2022 – *Charlie Grin / Charlie Grin “Sunflower”* (INFINIT3.io × Garageworks)

**Year:** 2022 (October 21, 2022)  
**Platform:** INFINIT3.io  
**Type:** Phygital release (paired physical + NFT collectibles)

![Charlie Grin / Sunflower Grin — INFINIT3.io](../images/Digital/charliegrin.webp)

## Overview

The **Charlie Grin / Charlie Grin “Sunflower”** release bridges Ron English’s iconic *Grin* character design with high-end phygital collecting. Produced in collaboration with **Garageworks Industries Ltd.**, the drop featured **609 total collectibles**, including:

- 9 unique **Charlie Grin** editions  
- 3 × 200 **Sunflower Grin** editions  

Collectors purchasing the physical sculptures received redemption codes unlocking matching NFTs via INFINIT3.io, creating a seamless digital–physical pairing. The drop was accompanied by a **Hong Kong physical activation at K11 MUSEA**, further cementing its hybrid format.

This release is notable for uniting designer-toy culture with blockchain verification and for expanding English’s *Grin* motif across both physical and metaverse contexts.
