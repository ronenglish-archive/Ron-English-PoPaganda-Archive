# 2024–25 – *VeVeVibes: Ron English — Delusionville Record Series*

**Years Active:** October 18, 2024 – June 21, 2025  
**Platform:** VeVe – VeVeVibes  
**Type:** Audio NFTs (music collectibles)  

![Ron English — Delusionville Record Series](../images/Digital/vevevibes.webp)

## Overview

The **VeVeVibes Delusionville Record Series** extends Ron English’s Popaganda universe into the realm of audio collectibles, pairing the surreal world of Delusionville with music from **The Rabbbits**, English’s long-running art-band project.

Across multiple drops—Records #1 through #5, the DJ Big Bots track, and the featured release **“Last Place is First Place”**—the series brings Delusionville’s chaotic morality tales into a new sonic format. Each audio NFT includes custom cover art, often featuring Rabbits, wolves, or Delusionville symbols rendered in psychedelic, high-contrast graphics.

Collectors view the series as the first major fusion of Ron English’s visual worldbuilding with his musical endeavors, forming a multimedia narrative bridge across the VeVe ecosystem.
