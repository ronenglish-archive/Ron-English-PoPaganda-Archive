# 2021 – *Slash × Ron English* (Nifty Gateway)

**Year:** 2021 (July 9, 2021)  
**Platform:** Nifty Gateway – Curated Drop  
**Type:** Multi-piece NFT collection (ranked auctions + small editions)

![Slash × Ron English — Nifty Gateway Collection](../images/Digital/slash.webp)

## Overview

The **Slash × Ron English** collaboration fused English’s Popaganda universe with the visual persona of legendary guitarist **Slash**. The drop introduced a lineup of surreal characters—**Falla Sheep, Punk Skunk, Montana Mouse, Forlorn Unicorn**, and the debut of **Riff Riff**, created specifically for this collaboration.

The release blended music culture, pop surrealism and collectible art in a way that resonated deeply with both Slash’s fanbase and English’s Popaganda collectors. It quickly became one of the most memorable crossover drops of early crypto-art, with several pieces selling through ranked auctions and limited editions.

This collaboration is often referenced as a bridge between English’s physical art legacy and a new era of digital pop-culture mashups.
