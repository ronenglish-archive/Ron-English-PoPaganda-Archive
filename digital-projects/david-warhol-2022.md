# 2022 – *VeVe Artworks: Ron English — David Warhol*

**Year:** 2022 (August 17–21, 2022)  
**Platform:** VeVe Artworks  
**Type:** 1-of-1 high-end digital artwork  

![Ron English — David Warhol (VeVe Artworks)](../images/Digital/davidwarhol.webp)

## Overview

**“David Warhol”** marks Ron English’s **first-ever 1-of-1 artwork** released through **VeVe Artworks**, the platform’s premium fine-art channel. The piece merges two giants of pop culture—Michelangelo’s *David* and Andy Warhol—into a single hybrid figure that embodies English’s trademark collision of high art and mass media.

Auctioned in a timed silent-bid format, the release positioned English within VeVe’s upper-tier art ecosystem, placing *David Warhol* alongside other blue-chip digital art offerings. Collectors emphasize this piece as a cornerstone for English’s presence in the high-end NFT art space.
