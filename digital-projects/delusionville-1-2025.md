# 2025 – *Ron English — Delusionville #1* (Digital Comic)

**Year:** 2025 (February 12, 2025)  
**Platform:** VeVe – Digital Comics  
**Type:** First dedicated *Delusionville* comic  

![Ron English — Delusionville #1](../images/Digital/delusionville1.webp)

## Overview

**“Delusionville #1”** marks the first fully dedicated comic set inside Ron English’s sprawling Delusionville universe—a world populated by Rabbits, wolves, suicidal unicorns, and other residents of English’s fractured fable of morality and pop culture.

Released as a blind-box NFT comic with four cover variants, the issue introduces the foundational moral conflict of Delusionville: hierarchical animal societies built on propaganda, self-delusion and distorted ethics. The comic deepens English’s long-running exploration of American myth-making through bright colors, fable logic, and razor-sharp satire.

Collectors highlight this release as a milestone: the moment Delusionville shifted from a recurring visual theme into a structured narrative world inside VeVe’s comic ecosystem.
