# *Viva la PoPaganda* ### La Bestia Thrash Art Gallery, Mexico City (2023) ### **Solo Exhibition** **Year:** 2023 **Dates:** June 2–4, 2023 **Venue:** La Bestia Thrash Art Gallery **Location:** Mexico City, Mexico **Title:** *Viva la PoPaganda* --- ## Overview Short-run solo event at **La Bestia Thrash Art Gallery** presenting **72 Ron English art toys**, with promotional artwork created by English exclusively for the event.
