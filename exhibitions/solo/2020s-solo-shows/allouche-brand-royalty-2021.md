---

## Overview

Solo exhibition premiering around **twenty new paintings** in which Ron English riffs on luxury branding, mascots and consumer icons. Working in densely layered canvases, English pushes his **POPaganda characters** into explorations of **power, status, and desire**, amplifying his long-running critique of consumer culture through high-gloss painterly techniques.
