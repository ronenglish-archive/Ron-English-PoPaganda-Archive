# *Vinyl Chord: A Revolutionary Record Store*
### Public Pool, Brooklyn (2023)

### **Solo Exhibition / Installation**

<img src="../../../images/Exhibitions/Solo/vinyl-chord.jpg"
     alt="Ron English — Vinyl Chord: A Revolutionary Record Store, Public Pool, Brooklyn, 2023"
     class="exhibit-image">

**Year:** 2023  
**Dates:** September 16 – October 1, 2023  
**Venue:** Public Pool (in collaboration with Ethan Cohen Projects and Founders Lab NYC)  
**Location:** Brooklyn, New York, US  
**Title:** *Vinyl Chord: A Revolutionary Record Store*

---

## Overview

Pop-installation and exhibition at **Public Pool** in Brooklyn, created in collaboration with **Ethan Cohen Projects** and **Founders Lab NYC**.  
Presented as a conceptual “record store,” the project installed vinyl artworks, customized record covers, POPaganda characters, and music-culture references, turning the space into an immersive hybrid of gallery, store, and counter-culture archive.
