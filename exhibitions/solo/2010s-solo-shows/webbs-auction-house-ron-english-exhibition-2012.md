# *Ron English Exhibition*  
### Webb’s Auction House & Gallery, Auckland, New Zealand (2012)

### **Solo Exhibition**
     
<img src="../../../images/Exhibitions/Solo/aukland.jpg"
     alt="Ron English — Exhibition at Webb’s Auction House, Auckland, 2012"
     class="exhibit-image">

**Year:** 2012  
**Dates:** May 10 – 20, 2012  
**Venue:** Webb’s Auction House & Gallery  
**Location:** Auckland, New Zealand  
**Title:** *Ron English Exhibition*

---

## Overview

Ron English’s **first New Zealand solo exhibition** at Webb’s Auction House & Gallery — part of the **Semi-Permanent** side programme — featuring paintings, limited editions, and installations that introduced his POPaganda aesthetic to audiences in Auckland.
