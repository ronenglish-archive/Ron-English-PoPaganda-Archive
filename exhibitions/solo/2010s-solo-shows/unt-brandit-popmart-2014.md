# *Brandit-Popmart*  
### UNT on the Square (University of North Texas), Denton, Texas (2014)

### **Solo Exhibition**
     
<img src="../../../images/Exhibitions/Solo/branditdenton.jpeg"

     class="exhibit-image">

**Year:** 2014  
**Dates:** March 4–8, 2014  
**Venue:** UNT on the Square (University of North Texas)  
**Location:** Denton, Texas, US  
**Title:** *Brandit-Popmart*

---

## Overview

Pop-surreal intervention and installation unpacking commercial iconography and **advertising language**, staged at the University of North Texas during a focused short run, linking English’s POPaganda practice to academic and student audiences.
