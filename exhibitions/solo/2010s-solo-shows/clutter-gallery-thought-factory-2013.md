# *Thought Factory*  
### The Clutter Gallery, Beacon, New York (2013)

### **Solo Exhibition**
     
<img src="../../../images/Exhibitions/Solo/Clutter Gallery Presents Ron English’s - Thought Factory, 2013 (1).png"
     alt="Ron English — Thought Factory at The Clutter Gallery, Beacon, NY, 2013"
     class="exhibit-image">

**Year:** 2013  
**Dates:** June 8 – July 6, 2013  
**Venue:** The Clutter Gallery  
**Location:** Beacon, New York, US  
**Title:** *Thought Factory*

---

## Overview

Original **drawings, studies, and toy-related process material**, focusing on the preparatory elements behind English’s POPaganda characters and sculptural editions, presented in Beacon’s designer-toy hub.
