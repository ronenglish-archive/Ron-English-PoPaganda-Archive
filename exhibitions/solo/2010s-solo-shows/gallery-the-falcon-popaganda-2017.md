## Overview

Exhibition featuring framed photos and **circus-style POPaganda banners** from **Delusionville**, presented at Gallery @ The Falcon in the Hudson Valley. The short-run show activated English’s performative universe of painted, sculptural and banner-based characters during late 2017, concluding with a New Year reception on January 6, 2018.
