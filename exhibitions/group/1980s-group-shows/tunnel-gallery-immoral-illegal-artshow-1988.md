# *The Immoral & Illegal Artshow*  
### Tunnel Gallery, New York (1988)

<img src="../../../images/Exhibitions/Group/immoral.jpg"
     alt="The Immoral & Illegal Artshow at Tunnel Gallery, New York, 1988"
     class="exhibit-image">

**Year:** 1988  
**Date:** July 6, 1988  
**Venue:** Tunnel Gallery  
**Location:** New York, New York, US  
**Show Title:** *The Immoral & Illegal Artshow*  

---

## Overview  

A group exhibition at **Tunnel Gallery** featuring downtown artists including Ron English. The show also presented a section of English’s **Texas pirate-billboard photography**, connecting his activist interventions and early billboard works to the New York scene.

The exhibition was part of the late-1980s downtown wave of alternative, artist-organized projects operating outside commercial gallery systems.
