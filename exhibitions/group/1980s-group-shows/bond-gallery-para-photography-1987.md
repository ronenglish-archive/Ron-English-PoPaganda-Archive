**Year:** 1987  
**Dates:** January 18 – February 12, 1987  
**Venue:** Bond Gallery  
**Location:** New York, New York, US  
**Title:** *Para Photography*  

---

## Overview  

*Para Photography* presented seven artists exploring experimental photographic processes and mixed-media image construction. Ron English contributed photographic works connected to his ongoing billboard interventions and evolving “popaganda” visual language during the mid-1980s.
