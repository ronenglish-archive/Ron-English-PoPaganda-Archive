**Year:** 1989  
**Date:** June 27, 1989  
**Venue:** Under Acme  
**Location:** New York, New York, US  
**Title:** *MGD — Painting N.Y.C.*

---

## Overview

A one-night downtown show at **Under Acme**, presenting a large lineup of New York alternative artists, with live music from **The Gooney Birds** and **DJ Junky Jeff**.  

Ron English participated among a long list of artists associated with the downtown experimental and Armamentariumist scene of the late 1980s.
