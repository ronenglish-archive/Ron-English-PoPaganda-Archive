---

## Overview

Summer group exhibition organized at the **Dance Theater Workshop Gallery** in 1989 as part of a two-stage seasonal presentation by **2 T.Q.**. The opening reception took place on **August 8**, and the exhibition contributed to Dance Theater Workshop’s mission of bridging performance communities with contemporary visual artists.

Ron English participated alongside other New York-based artists active in alternative spaces and nontraditional exhibition venues of the period.
